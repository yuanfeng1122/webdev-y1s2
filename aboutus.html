About us

Sports Direct is a British retail company founded by Mike Ashley in 1982.
Originally named Mike Ashley Sports, the company rebranded as Sports Direct in 2000.
The company has since become one of the largest sports retailers in the world

Our Team

At Sports Direct, we have a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about
sports and helping our customers find the right gear to meet their needs.

Our team includes experienced sales associates, product experts, and customer service
representatives who are always happy to assist you with your purchases.

We believe that our team is the key to our success, and we are committed to providing
ongoing training and support to help our employees grow and develop in their roles.

We are proud to have a diverse team that reflects the communities we serve, and we strive
to create an inclusive and welcoming workplace for all.

Whether you're looking for advice on the best running shoes for your next race or need help
finding the perfect gift for the sports lover in your life, our team is here to help.
Get to know us better by visiting one of our stores or contacting us online.
